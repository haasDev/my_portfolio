About Me

Hello. I came into the web industry a little by accident. What started for me as a means to solve a problem at work lead to something I now find extremely fun and rewarding. As I have gotten the opportunity to work with many different technologies I have developed a passion for working with the web. Only on the web can you find so many tools to find solutions to problems in new and exciting ways. Over the last three years I have had great opportunities to work on projects that have challenged my skills and helped me grow. Although I have loved building my own things and doing side projects at work, I would like to reach my goals of becoming a full-stack developer. If you are looking for a junior front-end developer please reach out. I look forward to talking with you!
